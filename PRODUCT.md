# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
General audience; SaaS founders, developers, and marketers looking for a premium landing page template to launch their AI automation products.

## Product Purpose
A high-end, high-converting landing page template. It needs to provide a strong structural foundation while allowing for easy customization. Success means the template looks expensive and professional right out of the box, avoiding the traps of typical AI product marketing.

## Brand Personality
Tech-minimal, premium, restrained, confident.

## Anti-references
- "AI slop": magenta/purple gradient explosions, meaningless glowing orbs, overused glassmorphism, glowing borders on everything.
- Generic SaaS clichés: The "hero-metric" block, generic illustrations.
- Cheap, overly busy template layouts that lack focal points.

## Design Principles
- **Restraint over decoration:** Use typography, scale, and spacing to create visual hierarchy instead of relying on excessive color or borders.
- **Quiet confidence:** A premium aesthetic doesn't need to shout. It relies on precise execution and high-quality fundamentals.
- **Structural clarity:** Because it's a template, the layout must be robust, logical, and easy for end-users to parse and adapt.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Standard WCAG AA contrast ratios.
- Semantic HTML structure.
